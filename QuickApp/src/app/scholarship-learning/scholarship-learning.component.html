Introduction to E-Learning

This module provides an overview of the basics of e-learning, including its benefits and key components. Explore the content below to get started.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understand the concept of e-learning.
  • Identify the benefits of e-learning.
  • Explore different types of e-learning content.

Content:

E-learning, or electronic learning, refers to learning conducted via electronic media, typically on the Internet. It includes various types of content such as videos, interactive quizzes, and textual information.

Benefits of e-learning include accessibility from anywhere, flexible scheduling, and often a more engaging learning experience through multimedia.
